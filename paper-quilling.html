What is Paper Quilling?

Paper quilling is a decorative art form in which thin strips of paper are rolled, shaped, and glued together to create beautiful designs. It is widely used in greeting cards, wall frames, and handmade gifts.

Steps to Create Paper Quilling

  1. Cut colored paper into thin strips
  2. Roll the strips using a quilling tool
  3. Shape the rolls into designs
  4. Paste them neatly to create art
